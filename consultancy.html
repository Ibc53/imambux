Consultancy

Research-informed support for agricultural projects, field studies, and extension programs

I offer consultancy grounded in agricultural extension practice, field-based research, and smallholder-focused inquiry, helping institutions design stronger studies, tools, and farmer-oriented interventions.

Services

Survey & Instrument Design

Questionnaire development, construct alignment, adaptation from prior literature, local-language support, pilot testing, and reliability-focused refinement for agricultural and social science research.

Field Research Planning

Support with sampling strategy, enumerator guidance, data collection workflow, consent procedures, and practical field implementation for rural and extension studies.

Data Analysis & Interpretation

Assistance with descriptive statistics, reliability analysis, regression, moderation analysis, visual presentation of findings, and writing research-based interpretations.

Farmer-Centred Extension Support

Input on farmer training content, innovation adoption studies, communication-channel analysis, and context-sensitive extension strategy development.

Best Fit For

  • Academic research projects in agriculture, extension, and rural development
  • NGO and development studies involving farmer knowledge, barriers, and behaviour
  • Graduate students needing support with questionnaires, methods, and analysis
  • Programs assessing training outcomes, adoption readiness, and communication pathways
  • Projects requiring clear, evidence-based interpretation of survey findings

Expertise & Tools

  • SPSS, AMOS, and R for applied analysis and visualization
  • Quantitative agricultural and extension research
  • Survey design, adaptation, and validation
  • Enumerator training and field deployment planning
  • Scientific writing, research presentation, and interpretation support

How I Work

  1. Clarify the project objective, research need, or field problem
  2. Design or refine the study tool, scope, and workflow
  3. Support implementation, data handling, and quality checks
  4. Analyse findings and turn them into clear, usable outputs
  5. Deliver practical recommendations for research, training, or program action
