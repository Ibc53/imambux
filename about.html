Imam Bux

I am a PhD Scholar at Universiti Putra Malaysia and former Lecturer at Sindh Agriculture University. My research focuses on agricultural technology adoption, rural development, and food security. I aim to bridge research and practice through teaching, surveys, farmer training, and consultancy.

Biography

I specialize in Agricultural Extension with expertise in technology adoption, farmer training, and impact evaluation. With experience as a Lecturer and researcher, I work across academia, industry, and community to deliver sustainable agricultural solutions. I am passionate about empowering farmers, supporting rural development, and advancing food security through innovative practices.
