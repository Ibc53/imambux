Why Community Matters to Me

My academic journey began in a rural and resource-constrained environment, so community engagement is not separate from my work. It is central to how I understand education, leadership, and agricultural development. Whether I am working with students, researchers, or farming communities, I believe knowledge becomes meaningful when it is shared, accessible, and rooted in service.
