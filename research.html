Research

I study how innovations spread among farmers and what accelerates adoption. My work blends surveys, statistics, and training to turn research into practical extension tools.

Current Projects

Adoption of Recommended Dairy Farming Practices

Exploring drivers, barriers, and extension touchpoints among small-scale dairy farmers in Sindh. Providing insights for policy and farmer training.

Circular Economy for Dairy Systems

Studying low-waste practices such as manure valorization and feed loops. Assessing readiness and links to climate and cost outcomes.

Validated Instruments for Agri-Tech Adoption

Designing reliable, farmer-friendly questionnaires for advisory services, mechanization, and precision agriculture adoption studies.

Methods & Tools

  • Survey design: cross-sectional, case studies, systematic reviews
  • Data analysis: SPSS (6+ years), R, GraphPad Prism
  • Fieldwork: enumerator training, pilot testing, quality assurance
  • Visualization: charts, graphs, statistical plots
  • Ethics: informed consent, data anonymization, secure storage
